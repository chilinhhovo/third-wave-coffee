The third wave reimagines coffee as craft. This global movement emphasizes origin, process, and aesthetic. Caf√©s highlight single-origin beans, pour-over rituals, and hand-thrown ceramics. Design is central. Interiors often feature raw wood, soft light, and minimal ornamentation, projecting values like care, sustainability, and intentional living.

From Copenhagen to Chiang Mai, a shared visual language has emerged. This movement is less about caffeine, and more about values: care, craft, and quiet connection.
